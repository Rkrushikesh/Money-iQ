How the Slow Plan Works:

Step 1: Enrollment Eligibility To take advantage of this plan, users can enroll after 6 months. This period allows us to internally process user transactions and create a personalized plan based on these transactions.

Step 2: Goal Explanation For enrollment, users must provide a detailed explanation of their goals. This includes:
. Specific goals to achieve in the next 6 months or a year.
. Any specific plans they are working on.
. Details of any loans taken from other banks.
. Financial knowledge and understanding, including budgeting, investments, and saving strategies.
. We will work to improve your financial understanding and correct any mistakes.

Step 3: Process Assistance Go through our process. We will assist you by refining questions based on your submissions and records to ensure we capture all necessary details.

Step 4: Data Processing We will work on your data, which requires some time. Even with the help of AI, our team ensures accuracy before finalizing your personalized plan.

Step 5: Payment and Plan Delivery Proceed to the payment section and complete the payment. Once done, your enrollment will be completed, and you will receive your personalized plan.

Step 6: Plan Tracking After enrollment, you can track your plan's progress, monitor your habits, and receive important updates related to your financial health.
